### Feature Request: Add "Fun Zone" Level 2 Tab

#### Objective

Add a new **Level 2** tab to the **Coordinate Systems** section named **Fun Zone**. This tab should provide an engaging collection of interactive games and activities that reinforce the core concepts taught throughout the Coordinate Systems module.

### Requirements

1. **Create a new Level 2 tab**

   * Add a new tab labeled **Fun Zone**.
   * Integrate it into the existing Level 2 navigation without affecting the behavior of the other tabs.

2. **Populate the tab with five activities**

   * Implement **five unique interactive activities**.
   * Each activity should be distinct in gameplay and learning style.
   * Activities may include games, timed challenges, puzzles, or other engaging interactive experiences.

3. **Learning objectives**

   * Every activity should require students to apply one or more of the key Coordinate Systems concepts, including:

     * Plotting and identifying points
     * Reading and interpreting coordinates
     * Translation
     * Rotation
     * Understanding coordinate relationships
   * The focus should be on applying concepts through gameplay rather than direct instruction.

4. **Activity design**

   * Each activity should:

     * Have clear instructions.
     * Provide immediate feedback to the student.
     * Include a measurable objective (score, completion, time, accuracy, etc.).
     * Encourage replayability through randomized content, increasing difficulty, or varied challenges.

5. **User experience**

   * Design the interface to be colorful, intuitive, and fun while remaining consistent with the application's overall visual style.
   * Allow students to easily select and switch between activities from within the Fun Zone tab.

### Goal

The **Fun Zone** should serve as the recreational component of the Coordinate Systems module, giving students an enjoyable way to reinforce and master key concepts through interactive play. The activities should prioritize engagement while remaining educational, encouraging repeated use and strengthening conceptual understanding through practice.
