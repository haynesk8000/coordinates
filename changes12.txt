Modify the **Explore** tab so the projectile's initial position coordinates (**x₀** and **y₀**) are represented graphically rather than numerically.

* Display the **x₀** indicator at the top of the visualization.
* Always display a **horizontal measurement line** extending from the projectile's initial position to the origin of the coordinate system.
* The length of the line should accurately represent the magnitude of **x₀** using the current coordinate scale.
* **Do not display a numerical value** for **x₀**.
* If the origin lies on a preset grid line, display the symbolic label **x₀** at the appropriate location.
* Otherwise, display only the label **x₀**, without any numerical value.

### y₀ Display

* Display the **y₀** indicator along the right side of the visualization.

* **Do not display a numerical value** for **y₀**.
* If the origin lies on a preset grid line, display the symbolic label **y₀** at the appropriate location.
* Otherwise, display only the label **y₀**, without any numerical value.

* The horizontal and vertical measurement lines must always be visible, regardless of the origin's position.
* Never display numeric coordinate values for **x₀** or **y₀**.

* Ensure the labels and measurement lines remain clear, unobtrusive, and visually consistent with the existing Explore tab interface.
